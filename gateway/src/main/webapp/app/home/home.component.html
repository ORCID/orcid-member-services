Welcome pilot participants!

The ORCID Member Portal is a new suite of tools intended to help institutions make the most of their ORCID membership. To make sure that it functions properly and supports members’ needs, we’re running a pilot test program, and we’re thrilled that your organization is interested in participating.

The Member Portal is under active development and will eventually include tools that allow you to:

  • Add/update data on researchers' ORCID records
  • Manage your organization's ORCID membership information
  • Manage your organization's ORCID API credentials
  • Access data and reports

Tools currently available for testing:

Add affiliations (working title! catchy name forthcoming) "Add affiliations" simplifies the process of adding affiliation information (employment, education, etc) to ORCID records by allowing you to add and update affiliation information on behalf of your researchers using a CSV file, without any need to integrate ORCID directly into your current systems or to acquire an ORCID-enabled vendor system.
